Welcome to PyHDL
================

PyHDL is a simple Hardware Description Language, meant for beginners to learn how to design hardware. 
To get started, head over to :ref:`installation` and then read the :ref:`quickstart`.

If you want to dive in to the internals of PyHDL, head over to the :ref:`api` documentation.
